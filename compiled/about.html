Who We Are

We're a dedicated team of researchers and engineers drawing from different backgrounds and ideas to push the boundaries of data intensive science. Our mission is to pioneer translational data intensive science to advance medicine and healthcare. We work closely with researchers at the University of Chicago and others around the world to pursue discovery and make an impact on society.
